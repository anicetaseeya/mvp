Got
Questions?
We got you.
Is this only for nightlife? +
Not at all. While many challenges happen at bars, we also have morning café quests, food trails, landmark challenges, and social missions across the whole city. If there's a partner venue involved, you can earn from it — day or night.
How do rewards actually work? +
Each challenge has a specific reward — a free shot, 10% off your round, free tapas, etc. Once your submission is approved you get a reward code. Show it to the bartender or staff and they honour it immediately. No waiting, no apps to scan.
Is it free? Do I need to buy anything? +
Playing is 100% free. Most challenges are set at partner venues and require you to order something — a drink, a dish — which is how you participate and unlock the reward. You're spending what you'd spend on a night out anyway, just getting more back from it.
How does the leaderboard work? +
Every approved submission earns XP. More XP = higher rank. The leaderboard resets weekly. Top 3 players each week earn exclusive bonus rewards from partner bars, announced every Monday.
Which venues are partner bars? +
We're signing up bars, restaurants, and cafés across Segovia right now. All active partner venues show up on the quest cards. More are added every week — if your favourite spot isn't on yet, it probably will be soon.
What if my submission gets rejected? +
You can resubmit immediately. Rejections happen when the proof doesn't clearly match the challenge — blurry photo, too-short voice note. Fix it and resubmit. Approvals usually land within minutes during active evening hours.
