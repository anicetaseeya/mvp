We Do Too.
Is Segovia Passport only for nightlife? +
Not at all. While some challenges happen at bars and clubs, many quests are about culture, food, landmarks, language, and social connection. You can explore the Roman Aqueduct, taste cochinillo, chat with locals, and much more — all while earning XP and rewards.
How do I earn rewards? +
Complete challenges and submit your proof (photo, voice memo, or text). Once an admin approves your submission, you earn XP, unlock a passport stamp, and receive your reward code to redeem at partner venues.
Is the app completely free? +
Yes — joining and playing is 100% free. Some challenges require a purchase at a partner venue (like ordering a drink) to unlock the associated reward, but you'll never pay us a cent.
How does the leaderboard work? +
Every approved challenge submission earns you XP. The more XP you collect, the higher you climb. Rankings reset weekly, giving everyone a fresh shot at the top spot each week.
Can I do challenges solo or only in groups? +
Most challenges can be done solo, though some are more fun with others. Group challenges are coming soon — where a squad can tackle a mission together and share the reward.
When are new quests added? +
New quests are added weekly, with special event quests on weekends and holidays. Follow us to get notified when fresh challenges drop — early completions sometimes earn bonus XP.
What if my submission gets rejected? +
No worries — you can re-submit. Rejections usually happen if the proof doesn't clearly match the challenge. Just try again with clearer evidence and it'll be approved.
