Got
Questions?
We Do Too.
Is Segovia Passport only for nightlife? +
Not at all. Challenges cover bars, restaurants, cafés, landmarks, language, and social experiences across the whole city. If a partner venue is involved, you can earn from it — morning coffee, lunch, or a night out.
How do rewards work — what do I actually get? +
Each challenge has a specific reward — a free shot, 10% off your round, a free tapa, and so on. After your submission is approved, you get a reward code to show the bartender or staff. They honour it on the spot. No apps, no waiting.
Is it free? Do I need to buy anything? +
Joining and playing is 100% free. Most challenges are set at partner venues and require you to order something (a drink, a dish) — that's how you participate and unlock the reward. You're spending what you'd already spend on a night out, just getting more back from it.
How does the leaderboard work? +
Every approved submission earns XP. More XP = higher rank. The leaderboard resets weekly so everyone gets a fresh shot. Top 3 players each week earn exclusive bonus rewards from our partner bars — announced every Monday.
Which bars are partner venues? +
We're signing up bars and restaurants across Segovia right now. All active partner venues are shown on the challenge cards. More are being added every week — if your favourite spot isn't listed yet, stay tuned.
Can I do quests with friends? +
Yes — some challenges are specifically designed for groups (like Bring the Squad or Bar Crawl). Solo players earn XP too, but group challenges tend to give better rewards and are obviously more fun.
What if my submission gets rejected? +
You can re-submit immediately. Rejections happen when the proof doesn't clearly match the challenge — for example, a blurry photo or a voice note that's too short. Fix it and resubmit — approvals usually land within minutes during active hours.
